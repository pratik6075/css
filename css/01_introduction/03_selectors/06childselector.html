child selector

  • the child combinator (>) is placed between two css selector
  • it matches only those element matched by the second Selector that are the direct chidren eliets matched by the first selector
  • Example:-
    • If, x, y and z are three HTML elements,
    • and z resides within start and end tag of y,
    • and y resides within start and end tag of x;
    • then y is called as a child of x; and z is called as a child of y.
